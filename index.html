The Great Migration




Between the beginning of the 20th century and 1970 millions of Black Americans moved from the south to other parts of the united states. They sought freedom from the barriers that held them back. They sought a better life. This is the part of the story of these people. Follow the trials that they and so many other black Americans had to face, and what many still face today.
