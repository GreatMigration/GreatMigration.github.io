No Escape




For some though, escaping the South didn't alleviate the racism that they were so desperately trying to escape in the South. All of the migrants in The Warmth of Other Suns faced incredible pain realizing that many of the things they had tried to escape to the North and West still existed.

Follow the stories of some of the struggles of Dr. Robert Foster to the West, Ida Mae Gladney in Chicago, and George Sterling in New York. If you would like to continue past their stories, click ‘More’ below.
